# Retail Digital Transformation Analysis

## Objective
To analyse the impact of digital transformation on business performance in UK and EU retail sector.

## Tools
- R
 
## Methods
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) including distribution checks and correlation analysis
- Fixed effects model
- Regression analysis
- Heteroskedasticity test
- Multicollinearity 

## Key Findings
- Profitability metrics (ROE, ROA, Profit Margin) show a clear peak around 2016.
- A significant decline is observed in 2020, likely due to external economic shocks.
- Recovery trend is visible post-2020, especially in ROE.

## Key Insights
- Digital adoption impacts business performance positively and statistical significially
- Stronger effects observed in large firms
